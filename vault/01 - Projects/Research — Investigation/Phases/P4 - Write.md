# P4 — Write

## Overview

Turn the synthesis into the deliverable in the project's declared `output_format`. Every load-bearing claim cites inline. Gaps are explicit. The piece is short enough to read in one sitting and long enough to defend.

## Tasks

- [ ] Read P1's scope, P2's source notes, P3's synthesis.
- [ ] Read [[08 - System/Principles/no-dated-citations-you-cant-pin.md|no-dated-citations-you-cant-pin]] — only year-stamp publications you fetched.
- [ ] Read [[08 - System/Principles/verifiable-contact-details-only.md|verifiable-contact-details-only]] — same logic for any contact details, helplines, deep URLs you'd publish.
- [ ] Draft the deliverable in the declared `output_format`:
  - **Report:** executive summary + sections per sub-question + gaps + recommendations.
  - **Brief:** one-page at most; angle + 3-5 findings + gaps + sources.
  - **Memo:** decision-oriented; recommendation up front; supporting evidence below.
  - **Dashboard:** numerical synthesis with chart references; supporting prose minimal.
- [ ] Cite every load-bearing claim inline in your project's chosen citation style (Vancouver, APA, footnote, hyperlink — whatever your domain expects).
- [ ] Name gaps explicitly in their own section. Don't bury them.
- [ ] Length-check: short enough to be useful, long enough to defend the load-bearing claims.

- [ ] Year-stamps only on publications actually fetched.
- [ ] Gaps section present and honest.
- [ ] Length proportional to the question.
