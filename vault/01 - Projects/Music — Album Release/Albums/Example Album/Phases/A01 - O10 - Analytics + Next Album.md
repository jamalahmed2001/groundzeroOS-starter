# A01 O10 — Analytics + Next Album Brief

## Overview

After ≥ 2 weeks of streaming data, look at what the album told you. Top track? Worst track? Geo split? Listener demographics? Pre-save → save conversion? Use the data to brief the next album.

## Tasks

- [ ] Wait ≥ 2 weeks post-release.
- [ ] Pull streaming data via the distributor / Spotify for Artists / Apple Music for Artists.
- [ ] Compute: streams per track, saves, skip rate, completion rate, listener demographics (geo, age, gender), playlist placements.
- [ ] Identify: top-performing track, worst-performing track, surprising finding.
- [ ] Update the album's `## Post-release` section.
- [ ] Write `Albums/<album>/Next Album Brief.md` — a 1-page brief for the next album: what the data suggests doubling down on, what to drop, what to test.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `## Post-release` populated with metrics.
- [ ] Next Album Brief written and grounded in data, not gut feel.
